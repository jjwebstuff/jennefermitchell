Services We Offer

Coaching
Jennefer has been coaching her clients through a co-relationship between Coach and Client(s). Consider Jennefer to be you "GPS" that will continue to hold the map while you navigate to your destination. Jennefer provides support and guidance and most of all ACCOUNTABILITY for you to achieve your goals. This is all accomplished through step by step planning that you define and develop with your GPS there at all times to help back you up. Helping professionals master change & put the spark back into their lives.
Consulting
Jennefer has been consulting with organizations and leading the client to the solution through her years of expertise in the industry. Her main focus has been within the "Automotive" industry representing several of the manufacturers and their dealer networks. She is known for her ability to hold the client accountable to their commitments and goals.
Training and Facilitation
Jennefer has been facilitating with a wide range of clients. Jennefer encourages everyone to participate during a facilitated session. There is great success when the team is cross-functional and exploring using their "Best Thinking." Jennefer helps the client identify the topic(s) and team(s) and she promotes "all ideas." Jennefer facilitates the team to learn through a structured four step process of finding, analyzing, discussing and implementing.
Group Coaching and Facilitation
Group coaching and facilitation is a great way to increase the richness in the team’s diversity through their energy and ideas. You will benefit from more “aha” moments where individual insights and solutions are highlighted based on other members who have experienced some of the same things. Jennefer is always amazed at how fast the team learns and grows from each other’s collective wisdom that derives joy from the “journey”. There is nothing better than group coaching/facilitation where members of the team feel safe to develop both personally and professionally.
